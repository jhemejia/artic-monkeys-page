Indie Rock from Sheffield

Arctic Monkeys is a British indie rock band formed in Sheffield in 2002. The band consists of Alex Turner (vocals, guitar), Jamie Cook (guitar), Nick O'Malley (bass), and Matt Helders (drums). The band's music is characterized by its raw, energetic sound and its use of unconventional instruments and recording techniques.
